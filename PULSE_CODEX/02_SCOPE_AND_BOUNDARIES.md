## What PULSE Does
- Ingests routine laboratory results (e.g., CBC).
- Aggregates and normalizes data by age and sex.
- Detects population-level signal deviations over time.
- Applies statistical early-warning models (EWMA, CUSUM, Farrington).
- Produces dashboards and analytical reports for experts.

## What PULSE Does NOT Do
- Does NOT diagnose individuals.
- Does NOT provide treatment or clinical recommendations.
- Does NOT replace LIS, EHR, or national reporting systems.
- Does NOT expose identifiable patient-level data.

## Intended Users
- Public health analysts
- Health system planners
- Research and policy experts

## Explicit Exclusions
- Individual clinical decision support
- Automated medical advice
- Real-time clinical alerts to patients or doctors
